Frequently Asked Questions

What does this site do?

Intrare is a platform that connects NFT communities with a global online marketplace. We help provide NFT utility by providing holders access to discounts, perks, and other benefits of NFT ownership.

Do you have an example of what you mean by utility?

Imagine you are a clothing brand and would like to offer a 10% discount to the World of Women NFT community. Utilizing Intrare, you would be able to offer NFT holders a unique code, good for 10% off in your store, once ownership is validated through our site. The code cannot be reused and it is only revealed if the NFT is in your account.

Will I have to connect to MetaMask?

Yes. We only request READ access which you approve when you slick "Sign". You do not interact with smart contracts on our website. Allowing us read access your wallet provides us the ability to read metadata from the public blockchain and validate NFT ownership.
